The Thought Leaders

Through our research and experience we are continuously developing diagnostic instruments and workshops to help you grow and develop. You will learn how to deal with barriers and setbacks and empower yourself with confidence to build a life with meaning and purpose.

Dr. Jacqueline Brassey

Jacqui is a ‘Pracademic’, she enjoys bringing practical experience together with academic insights. Her work is backed by science and 20+ years of professional experience in various business contexts including ten years at Unilever. She specializes in (Executive) Leadership Development, Neuroscience in Organizations, Learning, Diversity & Inclusion, Change Management and Health & Well Being. Together with Nick van Dam and Arjen van Witteloostuijn she recently published two books (in English and Dutch) on developing Authentic Confidence and Emotional Flexibility.

Jacqui is currently Chief Scientist and Director of Research Science People and Organization Performance at McKinsey & Company. She also is Adjunct Professor at IE University in Spain and a Fellow researcher at VU Amsterdam. Finally, she is proud to serve as a member of the Supervisory Board of Save the Children the Netherlands.

Jacqui has co-authored over 20 (Book)-chapters and articles and has degrees in Business and Medical sciences. She holds a Bachelor in International Business and Languages from Avans University of Applied Science, a Cum Laude Bachelor/Master in Organization Sciences from Tilburg University, a PhD in Business and Economics from Groningen University and a joint Master in Affective Neuroscience from Maastricht University.

Prof. Dr. Nick van Dam

Nick is an internationally recognized thought leader, advisor, researcher, facilitator and best-selling author of more than 25 books on Corporate Learning and Leadership Development. Dr. van Dam has over 25 years of business experience as a former Partner, Global Chief Learning Officer, HR-Executive, and Client Advisor at McKinsey as well as at Deloitte.

Nick is a Board Member at IE University (Spain), Chief of the Center for Corporate Learning Innovation and a member of the IE Center for Health, Wellbeing and Happiness. He is a Professor at IE University, Nyenrode Universiteit and the University of Pennsylvania. Dr. van Dam is the Academic Director of the IE Executive Master in ‘Positive Leadership and Transformation’ and the ‘International Masterclass L&D Leadership.’

He is affiliated with McKinsey & Company as an External Senior Advisor and Facilitator of Leadership Programs. Nick is the founder of the e-Learning for Kids Foundation, which has provided free digital lessons for more than 20 million underprivileged elementary school children.

Authentic Confidence

English

Reaching your full potential will take courage and authentic confidence. You can develop this courage by building the skills to deal with any situation that crosses your path in your career. This assessment will measure you on six dimensions of emotional flexibility and inflexibility in support of building authentic confidence. We use scales that are validated through research. For research purposes we also ask a few additional questions. You get the relevant references at the end of the survey. All results are anonymous and never shared at individual level beyond the researchers. You will receive the scores of your assessment per mail within 24 hours after completing the survey.

Authentiek Zelfvertrouwen

Dutch

Het bereiken van je volledig potentieel vraagt soms om moed en authentiek zelfvertrouwen. Je kunt dit zelfvertrouwen ontwikkelen door het werken aan vaardigheden die je helpen met diverse situaties die je kunt tegenkomen in je carrière om te gaan. Deze vragenlijst meet zes dimensies van emotionele flexibiliteit en inflexibilitet ter ondersteuning van authentiek zelfvertrouwen. We gebruiken vragenlijsten die door onderzoek gevalideerd zijn. Voor onderzoeksdoeleinden stellen we een aantal additionele vragen. Alle resultaten zijn anoniem en zullen nooit op individueel niveau gedeeld worden buiten de onderzoekers. U krijgt uw resultaten per mail binnen 24 uur nadat u de vragenlijst ingevuld en ingeleverd hebt.
